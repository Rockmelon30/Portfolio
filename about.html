About Me

Originally from Canada, I have been a resident of Los Angeles since 2017. I’ve always been passionate about healthcare, biology, nature, and technology, and with recent advancements in AI, I’m particularly excited about how these fields can intersect and create innovative solutions.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the opportunity to work across a diverse range of industries, including healthcare, automotive, firefighting, and the service industry. These experiences have shaped me into an adaptable and versatile professional, always eager to learn and take on new challenges.

In recent years, I dedicated my free time to learning how to code and have since completed the Data Science Bootcamp at General Assembly. My technical skillset includes Python, SQL, R, and Tableau, as well as proficiency in core data science and machine learning libraries like Pandas, Scikit-learn, Keras, and TensorFlow.

I’m excited to apply my knowledge in real-world projects and continue expanding my expertise in data science and machine learning.
